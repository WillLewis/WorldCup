STEP UP YOUR GAME

The US has some of the most passionate sports fans in the world. Yet we don't represent when it comes to the world cup. We think this is due in part to the fact that the information on soccer isn't as accesible as it is for other sports. And it certainly isnt as accessible as it is in other countries. This project seeks to change that.

World Cup Explained

in 3 minutes ...give or take:)

This is an open source project. If you are interested you can find the code HERE.

STAGE 1 OF 2: GROUP PLAY

8

groups battle in group play to be the top two teams which will advance. How are the top two teams decided? Each team plays three games. The team scores 3 points for a win and 1 point for a draw. Points are tallyed at the end. Tie breakers are decided by size of point spread - in other words, points scored vs points conceeded. The top 2 teams advance to the Knock-out Stage


STAGE 2 OF 2: GROUP PLAY

16

teams play in a single-elimination tournament. This is where the performance in Group Play pays off. The #1 team from each group is matched up with a #2 team from another group. The first phase of Knock-out stage is the Round of 16, where 8 games are carried out over 4 days. The next phase is Quarter Finals, consisting of 4 games over 2 days. Thats followed by Semi-finals which is 2 games over 2 days. Finally there is the 3rd place and first place game.
